Arroz Con Pollo

Description:

This classic dish appears throughout many regions of the Americas because of its universal appeal and the Iberian heritage shared among Latin American countries.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Rice
  • Chicken broth
  • Tomatoes
  • Peas
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Brown the chicken in a pan with oil.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic; cook until soft.
  3. Stir in rice, tomatoes, broth, and spices.
  4. Simmer until the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked.
  5. Add peas at the end and serve warm.
