Chicken Cacciatore

Description:

Chicken cacciatore is an Italian dish where chicken is simmered with tomatoes, onions, peppers, herbs, and sometimes wine. It's a hearty, rustic meal with rich flavors, often served with pasta or bread.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Italian herbs (like oregano or basil)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and brown the chicken.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic; cook until soft.
  3. Stir in tomatoes and herbs; season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Serve hot with pasta, rice, or bread.
